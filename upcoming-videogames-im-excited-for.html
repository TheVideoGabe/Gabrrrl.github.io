Upcoming Videogames I'm Excited For

Here are some games I cant't wait for.

Posted by Gabriel C. on September 17, 2021

Alan Wake Remastered [PC, PS5, XSX, PS4, XBO] – October 5

Alan Wake Remastered is finally coming to playstation. The developers hope the title does well in that console because of how much story driven games are celebrated in that communitty.

I'm very excited to play this game.

Far Cry 6 [PC, PS5, XSX, PS4, XBO, Stadia] – October 7

Farcry 6 is coming on october.

Ubisoft has done a really good job keeping the farcry franchise alive.

This will be the first farcry to come to ps5 and it's looking pretty good in terms of development.

Metroid Dread [Switch] – October 8

Metroid is finally coming back in the form of a 2.5D platformer.

This game is looking really good and will hopefully help introduce the franchise to many new Nintendo Switch owners who are not familiar with it.

Back 4 Blood [PC, PS5, XSX, PS4, XBO] – October 12

Back 4 Blood had a pretty great beta and I'm excited to see more post updates from the game.

I also feel like the game needs a little more polish in the rogue-lite aspect of the game with the cards that can modify the gameplay.

Jackbox Party Pack 8 [PC, PS5, XSX, PS4, XBO, Switch] – October 14

Jackbox Party Pack 8 is coming and hopefully the games are great.

I've enjoyed playing murder mystery party 2 a lot in the past so i hope we can get another great game like that once this comes out.

Demon Slayer: The Hikonami Chronicles [PC, PS5, XSX, PS4, XBO] – October 15

What can i say about this game?...

I really enjoyed the anime, my sister got even my mother to watch it, the gameplay looks pretty good and i'm excited for it!

Resident Evil 4 VR [Oculus VR] – October 21

This game is looking Amazing!

It's going to be a scary frantic mess and i'm here for it.

The Dark Pictures Anthology: House of Ashes [PC, PS5, XSX, PS4, XBO] – October 22

Chose your own adventure horror game franchise Dark Pictures has gotten better after Little Hope.

I'm excited to see where this new game is gonna go.

Marvel's Guardians of the Galaxy [PC, PS5, XSX, PS4, XBO, Switch] – October 26

Guardians of the galaxy by Square Enix...

Square Enix made The Avengers game which flopped so hard earlier this year mostly because they made it a game as a service when everyone was asking for a single player game.

So this new Marvel game has to redeem square enix or they might not be able to make future Marvel titles.

Mario Party Superstars [Switch] – October 29

I'm both excited and upset at Nintendo for this game.

On the one hand they have revamped clasic mario party minigames and game boards and even added online to them.

On the other hand I feel like all of this could've been dlc for the mario party game that's already on the Switch.
