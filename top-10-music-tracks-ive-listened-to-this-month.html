Top 10 Music Tracks I've Listened To This Month

Here are my most played songs this month.

Posted by Gabriel C. on September 16, 2021

#10 The Ballad of Mona Lisa by Panic! At The Disco

Panic! at The Disco is a great band, I listen to many songs from them but this one is a timeless classic.

#9 Say You Like Me by We The Kings

I really like this song. It's in the Alternative/pop genre area. I'm a sucker for acoustic.

#8 Here Without You by 3 Doors Down

I'm surprised this song is here. I didn't think I've listened to this song that much but - hey I'ts a great song-.

#7 Stacy's Mom by Bowling for Soup

It's a classic song, maybe I have this song in too many playlists that's why it's here. Very catchy.

#6 Chasing Cars by Snow Patrol

Chasing Cars is a really good song, gives me goosebumps sometimes.

#5 Stereo Hearts (Feat. Adam Levine) by Gym Class Heroes

Stereo Hearts is an amazing catchy song. I think I'll always have this on my playlists.

#4 Dear Maria Count Me In by All Time Low

I love the Pop-Punk genre and this song is one of the top among that genre for me.

#3 Little Talks by Of Monsters and Men

This song is so weird, yet so well put together. This is one of my favorite duet songs.

#2 Sex and Candy by Maroon 5

The original Music video for this song is better for sure but I prefer the maroon 5 cover of the song.

#1 I will follow You Into The Dark by Death Cab for Cutie

This song will forever be what I gravitate towards in a karaoke bar... If i ever go to one.

What I'm trying to see is that I love singing this song. Love the acoustic, the lyrics, I love everything about this song.
