My Top 5 YouTube Channels

I love watching YouTube here are some of my favorite channels.

Posted by Gabriel C. on September 09, 2021

Digital Foundry

Digital Foundry has some really great technical content that contrasts the capabilities of current and older hardware.

They have broken the news to many great stories in the gaming industry, and I hope they continue to do so.

Good Mythical Morning

GMM is a great morning youtube series, there is a lot fof creativity that goes into these shows or more specifically the food episodes.

CNBC

CNBC is a really great place to get informative videos and news.

They have some really good reports on current events

IGN

IGN covers everything from Pop culture to videogames.

They have a really good reach in the gaming industry as well, so sometimes they might even get dibs on showcasing gaming or movie embargos.

Spawn Wave

Spawn Wave or "News Wave" has the best coverage on all things gaming news.

So if you don't have the time to keep up with everything online this channel is a really great way to catch up or keep up.
