Movies I'm Excited For

Here are some movies I cant't wait for.

Posted by Gabriel C. on September 12, 2021

Venom: Let There Be Carnage

Venom 2 was actually pretty good. I saw it a day before opening day in 4DX.

I'm a big fan of the MCU, and although this one came from sony there was a mind blowing after credit scene at the end of the movie.

Eternals

I don't know much about this movie, but just like shang-chi and the legend of the ten rings I hope I love it after going in blind.

Spider-Man: Far From Home

This movie is this years most anticipated MCU movie, I'm very excited to watch it.

Far from home is one of my personal favorite MCU movies, I've seen it at least 15 times.

I have a feeling that this movie might become my new favorite after it releases.

Morbius

Morbius is another Sony centered MCU film.

This movie hasn't had much marketing, at least that I've seen. But the movie might be made inside the Sam Raimi spiderman universe. And that could be interesting.

Halloween Kills

The Halloween franchise comes back with Halloween kills the movie to end the latest reboot of the series.

To be honest I don't know what's going on with the plot so far, but I'm a fan of horror movies and hope this one is good.

Dune

Dune is getting rebooted, the sci-fi that has inspired all works after it.

From the trailers It's looking pretty good, and I might see it on opening day.

Ghostbusters: Afterlife

This new Ghostbusters movie looks pretty good. Hopefully It's better than the last reboot of the franchise.

I'm actually glad that they're going back to the original movies.

The Matrix 4 Ressurections

This movie is looking like a sequel and reboot situation much like the new suicide squad.

You will probably not need to watch the first movies to understand the plot.
