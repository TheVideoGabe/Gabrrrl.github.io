What Games Am I Playing Right Now.

Here are some games I'm enjoying right now.

Posted by Gabriel C. on September 11, 2021

Deathloop

Man, Deathloop is a masterpiece. It does the timeloop genre pretty well.

I recommend this game to anyone that loves the detective genre, first person shooter games and something along the lines of the souls games.

Ghost of Tsushima: Director's Cut

This game was amazing, I finished the original release on PS4 and it got even better on PS5.

The gameplay is really good and the story and community for this game has been something to behold.

Dead by Daylight

A game for horror fans by horror fans. They just added Hellraiser to the collection of iconic horror movie killers.

This game is great to play with friends, and even better to hunt them down.

Returnal

Returnal came from the left field. But It piqued my interest because of the rogue-like aspect, and the 3D audio.

The game is great overall but the fact that you can't save or pause the game makes it hard to pick up casually.
