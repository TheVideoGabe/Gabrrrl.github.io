Some Songs I'm Learning To Play

Learning to play the guitar has taught me to be patient with the learning process

Posted by Gabriel C. on August 24, 2021

Riptide by Vance Joy

I gravitate towards this song because of the acoustic sound.

The melody is really nice, and playing this song helps with my rythm when I practice.

Can't Help Falling In Love by Elvis Presley

I really love this song, and It's a great song to practice as a beginner too because It's slow.

Although it gets hard if you can't play bar chords like I did when I started.

Closing Time by SemiSonic

This is a timeless clasic and the chord progression is really good on the acoustic guitar.

I have been practicing this song to change between chords faster.

She Will Be Loved by Maroon 5

This song is also really good for practicing rythm

I also love the chorus, when I play it on my guitar.

Say You Won't Let Go by James Arthur

This is a slow song that I really love.

I could probably play this one all day long.
