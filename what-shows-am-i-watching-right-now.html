What Shows Am I Watching Right Now

I love watching reality TV.

Posted by Gabriel C. on September 15, 2021

Survivor

Survivor is back! And so far the game is going to be fast and furious.

Every episode I've seen so far feels like to because of the new rules.

I'm really excited for this new season.

Big Brother

Big Brother came and went unlike other seasons, this time around the winner was African American and the way it happened was pretty crazy.

The Circle

The circle Season 1 was the cringiest thing I've seen in reality shows in a while. But Season 2 fix that a little.

Season 3 was actually pretty enjoyable, and the twists that they introduced this season made a lot of sense and were pretty funny.

Big Bang Theory

Me and my wife have been rewatching this show because we couldn't keep up with it when it was airing.

We're also pretty excited to see the sheldon cooper prequel in the near future.

Mythic Quest

A show about game developers that actually uses tech that's related to the industry and has meta commentary on gaming? Sign Me Up!

The last Tech related show I watched completely was the Silicon Valley sitcom, I was pretty bummed that the show got canceled.
